## CONNECTION REQUEST GUIDANCE

Max 300 characters. Lead with relevance — they're in demand gen, you're talking about leads. Don't pitch the whole product. Get a crack in the door.

**Example structure (rewrite every time):**
```
Hey [First Name], saw you're running demand gen at [Company] — wanted to connect. We help B2B tech teams get verified MQLs on a pay-per-accepted-lead model. Happy to share more if relevant.
```

**What NOT to do:**
- Don't open with "Hope this finds you well" or any filler
- Don't name-drop logos in a connection request — save for follow-up
- Don't ask for a call in the first message

---

## FOLLOW-UP MESSAGE GUIDANCE

Short. Lead with the guarantee. Offer the free ICP assessment as a zero-risk entry point.

**Example structure (rewrite every time):**
```
Hey [First Name], good to connect.

Quick context on what we do — OpGen runs content syndication campaigns for B2B tech companies on a pure CPL model. You define your ICP, we deliver leads who match it and engaged with your content. You approve every lead before you pay.

Happy to do a free ICP assessment — we'll tell you exactly how many people in our network match your parameters before you commit. Worth a look?
```

**What NOT to do:**
